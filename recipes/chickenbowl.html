Chicken Burrito Bowl

Grilled Chicken, Rice, Black Beans and Avocado; What's not to love.

Once you get used to making your burrito bowls at home you will skip chipotle,save money and have a much better and healthier meal. The chicken for this burrito bowl recipe is so juicy and delicious.

This recipe is so easy to make it will be a perfect lunch or dinner the entire family can enjoy. Plus it's a great meal to use for meal prepping.

Ingredients

  • 2 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • 1 Cup Cooked Brown Rice
  • 1 Tbsp Lime Juice
  • 1 Small Avocado
  • 2 Cups Mixed Greens
  • 1/2 Cup Black Beans
